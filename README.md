## Overview
This project is a competitive advertising analysis of major EdTech platforms.  
The objective was to study their paid ad strategies, messaging, strengths, and gaps.

## Competitors Analyzed
- upGrad
- Great Learning
- Simplilearn
- Coursera
- Jaro Education

## Tools Used
- Meta Ad Library
- Google Search Ads
- PowerPoint for presentation

## Key Insights
- Most brands focus heavily on salary growth and career outcomes.
- Strong use of university and tech partnerships.
- Repetitive messaging across competitors.
- Limited emotional storytelling.

## Opportunity Areas
- Use real learner success stories.
- Build aspirational and differentiated messaging.
- Create stronger brand positioning beyond price and certifications.
